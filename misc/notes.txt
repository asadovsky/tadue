Port routing:
 - Figure out internal IP:
     ifconfig | grep 192  ==>  inet addr:192.168.1.113
 - Forward port in router:
     Go to 192.168.1.1 (username="", password="admin")
     Go to "Applications & Gaming" tab
     Add single port forward (in=8080, out=8080, ip=113)
 - Get public IP:
     https://www.google.com/search?q=ip  ==>  98.248.42.191
 - Set IPN url to IP:
     http://98.248.42.191:8080
